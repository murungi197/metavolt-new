Our Journey

2023

Founded

METAVOLT TECH UG was founded in Kampala, Uganda, with the vision of providing innovative technology solutions to businesses and individuals. The company was established under the leadership of Tumwesige Nelson, the Chief Executive Officer (CEO), whose goal is to bridge the gap between modern digital technology and local businesses.
Supporting this vision is Kaweesi Sufian, the Manager, who helps oversee daily operations and ensures the company delivers high-quality services to its clients.
The Human Resources department, led by Murungi Innocent, focuses on building a strong and innovative team that drives the company forward.
Together, the leadership team works to provide services such as web development, IT support, and digital solutions, helping businesses grow and succeed in
the modern digital world while contributing to the advancement of technology in Uganda.
